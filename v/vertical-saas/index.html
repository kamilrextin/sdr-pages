WHY BLAST-MARKETING BREAKS FOR VERTICAL SAAS

Why blast-marketing breaks for vertical SaaS

Finite TAM

Total addressable market is 5K-50K buyers, not 5M. You can't blast paid like a horizontal SaaS without burning the list. The math demands a different motion.

Buyer behavior is offline

Trade shows, word-of-mouth, association lists. Paid search has tiny but high-intent volume. Most agencies optimize for the wrong inputs because they're easier to measure.

Sales is consultative and long

Marketing isn't asked for 'leads' — it's asked for qualified named-account meetings. Pipeline math is per-account, not per-lead.
